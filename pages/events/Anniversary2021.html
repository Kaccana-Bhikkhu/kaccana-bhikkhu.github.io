Abhayagiri 25th Anniversary Retreat – Nine Days of Dhamma
Ajahn Pasanno, Ajahn Amaro, Ajahn Sudanto, Ajahn Karuṇadhammo and Ajahn Ñāṇiko
Jun. 9, 2021 to Jun. 17, 2021
Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery in Redwood Valley, California
2 sessions, 25 excerpts, 1:20:58 total duration

In the midst of the coronavirus pandemic, the senior monks of Abhayagiri Monastery offered a nine-day hybrid retreat to celebrate its 25th anniversary. Only the two Q&A sessions hosted by Ajahn Pasanno are currently included in the Archive.
