Developing Skill in Reflective Meditation
Ajahn Pasanno, Ajahn Karuṇadhammo and Ajahn Ñāṇiko
Upāsikā Day, Dec. 1, 2019
Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery in Redwood Valley, California and online
2 sessions, 20 excerpts, 50:07 total duration

Luang Por Pasanno, Ajahn Karunadhammo and Ajahn Nyaniko lead an exploration of the Buddha’s teachings on bringing a specific theme into the mind for reflection during meditation, including choosing and developing fruitful themes and the benefits of this skill.
