28th Anniversary Practice and Study Day
Abhayagiri Saṅgha
Abhayagiri Anniversary event, Jun. 1, 2024
Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery in Redwood Valley, California and online
2 sessions, 26 excerpts, 1:21:59 total duration

Abhayagiri Buddhist Monastery commemorated its 28th anniversary with a Practice and Study Day on the theme of mindfulness of breathing and the contemplation of death. That evening, senior monks offered reflections on the history of Abhayagiri.
